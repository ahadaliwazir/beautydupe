Each sheet mask is soaked in a concentrated dose of PITERA™ — SK-II's signature ingredient derived from a natural fermentation process. It closely resembles the skin's Natural Moisturizing Factor (NMF), which means your skin absorbs it incredibly efficiently. The result? Deeply hydrated, luminous skin that looks like you just walked out of a professional facial.

## How to Use Them

Using the masks is simple:
1. Cleanse your face and apply your toner
2. Unfold the mask and place it over your face, aligning the eye and mouth openings
3. Leave it on for 10–15 minutes
4. Remove the mask and gently pat the remaining essence into your skin — no rinsing needed
