## Texture and Application

Despite containing Shea Butter and Jojoba Oil, QUENCH is surprisingly lightweight. It doesn't have the thick, paste-like consistency of traditional dry-skin creams. Instead, it glides on smoothly, sinking into the skin within a minute. 

Because it absorbs so well, it functions beautifully as a morning moisturizer under makeup, preventing foundation from clinging to dry patches or flaking throughout the day. It is also designed to be used on both the face and the neck.
